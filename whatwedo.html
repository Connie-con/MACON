What We Do

We work hard every day to give stray dogs and cats a healthy start in life, the opportunity to find loving homes, and protection from harm. When crises strike, and animals are most vulnerable, we are always among the first to respond.

We ensure the unique needs of stray animals are met and their voices are heard. We deliver lasting results for thousands of animals, including those hardest to reach.

Rescue and Relief

When disasters strike, we ensure the safety and rescue of stray dogs and cats.

Health and Nutrition

We improve the health and well-being of stray dogs and cats through medical care and proper nutrition.

Adoption and Shelter

We create greater access to safe shelters and loving homes for stray animals.

Animal Rights & Protection

We ensure that stray dogs and cats are protected from harm and cruelty.
